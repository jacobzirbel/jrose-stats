The 151

A community-built record of all 151 runs — logged from the videos and verified before anything is published.

1

Two people log each run independently. Watching the video, they tag events, gyms, and details on their own — without seeing each other's work.

2

The two logs are matched. Everything both loggers agree on becomes the canonical record and the run goes live.

3

Disagreements get one reconciliation round. The loggers fix their own logs and resubmit; if they still don't agree, an admin settles it.

Want to help? Open any Pokémon, find a video with an open slot (fewer than 👤 2/2 loggers), and log it — a second logger is all a run needs to get verified.
